Welcome to The Golden Acacia Boutique Hotel

Located in the heart of Machakos, The Golden Acacia Boutique Hotel offers a unique blend of luxury, tranquility, and authentic Kenyan culture. Our boutique hotel is set amidst beautiful natural landscapes, perfect for those seeking peace and elegance. Experience the charm of the hills, the serenity of the surroundings, and the warmth of local hospitality.
